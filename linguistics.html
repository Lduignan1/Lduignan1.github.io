My Focus: LLM Data & Evaluation

My work centers on the critical challenge of ensuring quality in large language model development. As LLMs become increasingly central to NLP applications, the importance of rigorous data collection and systematic evaluation has never been greater.

I specialize in:

  • Data Collection Pipelines – Designing and implementing systems for gathering high-quality training data
  • Data Quality Assessment – Developing metrics and processes to ensure data integrity and representativeness
  • Model Evaluation – Creating comprehensive evaluation frameworks to assess LLM performance across diverse tasks
  • Benchmark Development – Contributing to evaluation standards that measure what matters

Multilingual NLP
